The Korea Economic Daily (한국경제) covered our **multimodal EEG–fNIRS foundation model**, implemented for the first time worldwide. Pretrained on ~1,250 hours of brain-signal data from 918 participants via unsupervised learning, the model achieves high accuracy with only a small amount of labels and performs both single-modal and multimodal analysis within one model.
